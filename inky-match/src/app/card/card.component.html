Welcome to Inky Matching!

Rules:

The game will start with the cards placed face down. The player chooses a card and carefully turns it over. Be sure not to bother the surrounding cards. The player then selects another card and turns it over. If the two cards are a matching pair for example two Jacks then they will leave the cards facing up. If the cards are not a match they are turned back over and the player continues to try to find a match.
